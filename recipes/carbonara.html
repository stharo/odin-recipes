Ingredients

  • Olive or vegetable oil
  • Parsley
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Spaghetti
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Sausage
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Eggs

Instructions

  1. Boil water and cook spaghetti
  2. Put spaghetti aside, saving some spaghetti water
  3. Heat up the oil
  4. Add garlic
  5. Once browned, add sausage
  6. Add Minced Onion
  7. Once cooked, add spaghetti
  8. Add salt and pepper
  9. Lower the heat, add egg, parsley, and cheese
  10. Mix well and serve!
